    How does Risk Score work?

    Step 1 – Features (7 inputs to ML):

    • ip_new – New IP? (0 or 1)
    • location_new – New location? (0 or 1)
    • device_new – New device? (0 or 1)
    • time_deviation – Outside usual login hours? (0–1)
    • password_attempts – 1 + failed attempts in last 24h
    • failed_attempts_24h – Count of wrong passwords in last 24h
    • geo_distance – Distance from usual location

    Step 2 – ML model (Random Forest) predicts risk 0–100%.

    Step 3 – Decision:

    • Risk < 30% → Allow (dashboard)
    • 30% ≤ Risk < 70% → Adaptive question
    • Risk ≥ 70% → Blocked

    Step 4 – After success: Failed attempts are cleared. Next login uses 0 failed attempts → lower risk.
